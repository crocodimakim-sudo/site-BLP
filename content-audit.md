## 8. Brand Voice Guidelines (for writers)

**When writing for BLP Board:**

✓ **Do:**
- Use active voice ("We certify…" not "Certified…")
- Lead with benefits, then features
- Be specific (use numbers: "3050 mm", "150 cycles", "K0")
- Speak to each persona's actual pain point (architects want design freedom, developers want on-time delivery)
- Use short sentences (max 15 words)

✗ **Don't:**
- Use Russian jargon without explanation (НГ, К0, ГОСТ)
- Lead with features ("no asbestos" vs. "eco-friendly, proven safe")
- Use passive voice or vague terms ("reliable", "available", "support")
- Assume readers know the product category (some visitors are learning about fibre cement for the first time)

**Example:**
```
❌ Bad:  Панели отличаются высокой прочностью на изгиб и совместимостью с подсистемами.
✓ Good: Panels flex without cracking under load. Works with any subsystem you choose.
```
