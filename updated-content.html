Final Notes

Testing Recommendations

  • A/B Test Hero CTA: "Get a Quote" vs. "Get Expert Advice" vs. "Schedule Consultation" — measure form submissions
  • A/B Test Contact Form Title: "Get a Quote" vs. "Request Consultation" — measure form abandonment

Brand Voice Guidelines

  • ✓ Use active voice ("We certify…" vs. "Certified…")
  • ✓ Lead with benefits, then features
  • ✓ Be specific (use numbers: 3050 mm, 150 cycles, K0)
  • ✓ Speak to each persona's pain point
  • ✗ Avoid Russian jargon (НГ, К0, ГОСТ) without explanation
  • ✗ Don't use passive voice or vague terms
